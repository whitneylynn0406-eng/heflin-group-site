What You'll Build — Live, Over Two Days

Every concept taught, then shown live — then you build it yourself.

Choose your format: watch each concept get taught and demonstrated live, no laptop required — or build your own alongside as we go, which is highly encouraged, since that's where it actually clicks. Either way, here's what gets built over the two days:

  • Your own personal "chief of staff" AI assistant, configured around your real life and work
  • A "Morning Brief" agent — run it on demand, plus the exact playbook to make it run itself, automatically, every morning
  • A reusable skill you can invoke on demand, for anything you do repeatedly
  • The underlying pattern beneath all three — so you can build the next one yourself, for any task, without an instructor

Building along? You'll need a laptop (Windows or Mac) with admin rights and your own Claude Code subscription — not included in the course fee. Whatever recurring task you bring, sanitize it of any confidential data first (client data, patient data, proprietary business data — whatever applies to your own work) and use sample or placeholder data for the hands-on exercises instead.

Just watching and learning? None of that setup is required — show up and follow along. (Though, real talk — building along is more fun. Just saying.)
